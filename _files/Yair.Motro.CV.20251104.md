- **One‑Health WGS Analyses** – Comparative genomics of isolates from clinical, environmental (wastewater), and livestock sources to test epidemiological hypotheses.  
- **Targeted Microbiome Research** – QIIME2/Mothur pipelines for human, animal, and water samples; downstream R/Python analytics.  
- **Untargeted Metagenomics** – Shotgun data processing, custom pipeline development, and reproducible Jupyter/Rmarkdown notebooks.  
- **Microbial Bioinformatics Tools & Pipelines** – Development of detection, phylogeography, resistome, GWAS/pWAS, plasmid, simulation, and genome‑editing pipelines; collaboration on adversarial‑ML datasets with BGU CS.
